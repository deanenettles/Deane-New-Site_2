About Deane Nettles

Deane Nettles is a graphic designer and illustrator with decades of experience creating compelling visual communications for clients across diverse industries.

With a passion for typography, illustration, and design thinking, Deane brings a unique perspective to every project—whether it's a brand identity, publication design, advertising campaign, or custom illustration.

Based in Baltimore, Deane has worked with clients ranging from national magazines and non-profit organizations to local businesses and cultural institutions.

When not designing, Deane can be found teaching graphic design, creating art and sculpture, or dancing to live music.
